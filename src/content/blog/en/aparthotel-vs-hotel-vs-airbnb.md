Choosing your accommodation in Pals directly affects the quality of your stay. Between aparthotel, classic hotel and Airbnb rental, each option has its strengths and limits. Here is an objective comparison to help you decide according to your profile, budget and expectations. The [Aparthotel Arenal](/en/), located in the heart of [Pals](/en/blog/guide-complet-pals), illustrates the aparthotel format and its concrete advantages.

## Understanding the three formats

Before comparing, let us clearly define each option:

The classic hotel: rooms with complete hotel services (24/7 reception, daily cleaning, breakfast or half-board, sometimes restaurant, spa, pool). Ideal for short stays without logistics.

The aparthotel: equipped studios or apartments (kitchenette, bathroom, separate living space) with certain hotel services (reception, periodic cleaning, sometimes shared pool). Hybrid positioning between hotel and apartment.

Airbnb/VRBO rental: apartment or house rented from a private individual via a platform. No hotel service, total autonomy, great quality variability depending on the property.

Each format meets different needs. The right choice depends on the length of stay, the size of the group, the budget and the level of comfort sought.

The aparthotel sits in a competitive price zone, close to Airbnb, well below the 4-star hotel, while retaining hotel services absent from private rentals.

## The aparthotel: the service-autonomy balance

The strengths of the aparthotel:

Equipped kitchen: allows cooking local market products, quiet breakfast in the studio, picnics for the beach. Savings of 30 to 50 percent on the catering item.

Space: studios of 25 to 40 m², often with living area, terrace or balcony. More spacious than a hotel room for a similar price.

Hotel services: professional reception welcome, simplified key handover, mid-stay cleaning, linen provided, wifi, local advice from staff.

Flexibility: short stays (2-3 nights) or long ones (1-2 weeks) accepted, unlike some Airbnbs that impose a minimum of 5-7 nights.

Collective facilities: sometimes pool, private parking, bike room. At the Aparthotel Arenal: outdoor pool, free parking, shared terrace.

The limits: no restaurant on site (but Pals has excellent tables 2 minutes walk away), no daily cleaning (every 3-4 days depending on formula), entertainment and spa absent.

## The classic hotel: full services, higher cost

The strengths of the hotel:

Integrated services: daily cleaning, possible room service, restaurant on site, concierge, luggage storage. Zero logistical effort.

Buffet breakfast: included or optional, hearty, convenient for families. Morning time savings.

Entertainment and leisure: spa, pool, children's activities, clubs, sometimes private beach for seafront hotels. Attraction for short relaxing stays.

Reassuring credibility: certifications, stars, international standards. Security for those less used to Spain.

The limits: higher budget (often 1.5 to 2 times an equivalent aparthotel), smaller rooms, cooking impossible (except junior suite), less authenticity. Few hotels in Pals itself (the offer concentrates in Playa de Pals and Torroella).

## Airbnb: autonomy and authenticity

The strengths of Airbnb:

Potentially lower budget: especially off-season or for large groups (a 4-bedroom villa often costs less than a family hotel suite).

Local immersion: apartment in historic centre, house with garden, authenticity of a real Spanish home.

Space: for large families or groups, Airbnb villas offer surface and grounds few hotels propose.

Varied amenities: some properties include private pool, BBQ, garage, washing machine, which can make the difference for a long stay.

The limits: quality highly variable between hosts (from excellence to total disappointment), no hotel service, hidden fees (cleaning, platform, tourist tax sometimes to be paid separately), sometimes complicated check-in, difficult complaints in case of problem, local regulation tightening seasonal rental rules (some Airbnb accommodations in Pals risk being prohibited in the short term).

In Pals, the town hall tightened the regulation of tourist rentals in 2025, several accommodations lost their licence, creating last-minute cancellations for some travellers.

## Which accommodation for which profile?

Recommendations by traveller type:

Romantic weekend couple: 4-star hotel with spa if budget substantial, aparthotel if you favour local cooking and autonomy.

Family with children (full week): aparthotel ideal, the kitchen avoids restaurants every evening, the living area separates parents and children, shared pool.

Group of friends long stay: aparthotel or Airbnb villa depending on size (above 6 people, Airbnb becomes advantageous).

Remote worker or workation 2-4 weeks: aparthotel without hesitation, the equipped studio offers comfort and autonomy, the professional reception helps in case of problem.

Cycle tourist or hiker: aparthotel (often with bike room and drying room) or sports hotel if high budget. Avoid 3rd-floor Airbnb without lift.

Short stay 1-2 nights: hotel or aparthotel, not Airbnb (cleaning fees proportionally too high).

## The specific case of Pals

Pals is a medieval village of 3,000 inhabitants. The accommodation offer is specific:

Hotels in the village itself: no hotel in the historic centre (protected architecture). Hotel offer is in Playa de Pals (4 km from the sea) or neighbouring villages.

Aparthotels: a few aparthotels including the Aparthotel Arenal, located at the foot of the medieval village. Ideal compromise between centre and nature.

Airbnb villas: numerous around Pals (rural area), but often far from centre. Car essential. Quality highly variable.

Tourist residences: a few recent residences oriented towards mid-range holidaymakers.

## Frequently asked questions on accommodation choice

### Is an aparthotel worse than a hotel?

No, it is a different format. A 3-star aparthotel often offers more space than a 3-star hotel, but fewer daily services. The choice depends on what you value: services (hotel) or autonomy and space (aparthotel).

### Is Airbnb always cheaper?

Not systematically. In high season, cumulative fees (cleaning 80-150 euros, platform 15 percent, tourist tax) can bring the price close to an aparthotel without the services. Always compare the final price after taxes.

### Can you cook in an aparthotel?

Yes, this is the essence of the format. Studios are equipped with a kitchenette (hobs, microwave or oven, fridge, crockery). This allows alternating home cooking and restaurants.

### Is there a night reception in an aparthotel?

Variable depending on the establishment. The Aparthotel Arenal offers daytime reception and a 24/7 emergency number. Hotels of 3 stars and above generally provide night presence.

### Is Airbnb legal in Pals?

Seasonal rentals must have a tourist licence (HUTG for Pals). In 2025, the town hall revised licences: several accommodations are no longer compliant. Always check for the HUTG number on the listing before booking.

### Which format for remote work?

The aparthotel offers the best compromise: professional wifi, workspace, basic services, flexibility on duration. Some offer advantageous weekly or monthly rates.

### Can I cancel easily?

Hotel and aparthotel generally offer standard conditions (free cancellation up to 48-72h before). Airbnb depends on host policy (sometimes strict without refund). Prefer flexible options if your planning is uncertain.
