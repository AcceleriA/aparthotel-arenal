The Empordà has established itself as one of the Mediterranean's most prestigious golf destinations. Located in the heart of [Costa Brava](https://www.costabrava.org), Pals is not merely an iconic medieval village but also an ideal base for golf enthusiasts. From the [Aparthotel Arenal](/en/), you'll have easy access to several championship courses that combine exceptional design with spectacular Mediterranean vistas.

## Golf de Pals: The Legendary Costa Brava Course

[Golf de Pals](https://www.golfpals.cat) is undoubtedly the region's most emblematic course. Located just three minutes' drive from Aparthotel Arenal, this 18-hole course was designed by legendary British architect Donald Harradine in 1966. The design has been refined over decades, positioning it among Spain's top ten courses according to international rankings.

What distinguishes Golf de Pals is its extraordinary integration with the natural landscape. The holes wind through ancient rice paddies with views of the Mediterranean and the Illes Medes. The course presents considerable technical challenge, with strategically placed bunkers and greens that demand precision. Experienced players will particularly appreciate holes 6, 14, and 17, which combine visual beauty with tactical difficulty.

The club boasts an exceptional golf school offering personalized lessons and improvement programs for players of all levels. Facilities include a spacious practice range, putting green, short-game area, and restaurant with a terrace overlooking the course.

## Golf d'Empordà: Modern Challenge in Rural Heart

Just twenty minutes' drive away, [Golf d'Empordà](https://www.golfemporda.com) represents an interesting contrast. Inaugurated more recently, this 18-hole course was designed according to contemporary principles of sustainability and accessible play. While Golf de Pals captivates through history, Golf d'Empordà stands out for its modernity and tactical flexibility.

This course offers multiple tee options to accommodate different skill levels, from beginners to professionals. Holes are designed with generous landing zones but require precision at the greens. Native vegetation, including cork oaks and pines, provides authentic natural setting without sacrificing accessibility.

Golf d'Empordà also deserves special recognition for its environmental preservation commitment. The course is part of ecotourism initiatives in [Catalonia](https://www.catalunya.com), utilizing sustainable irrigation systems and maintaining protected natural habitats.

## PGA Catalunya: Championship Resort Experience

For golfers seeking a more comprehensive experience, the PGA Catalunya resort, located in Caldes de Malavella (approximately forty minutes' drive), offers two world-class championship courses. While requiring a longer excursion from Pals, it's worthwhile for those wishing to play courses that have hosted first-level professional tournaments.

## Practicing Around Pals

Beyond these main courses, the region offers practice options and 9-hole courses. Access to these facilities is particularly useful for warming up before tackling Golf de Pals' challenges or for practicing specific shots without committing an entire day.

Many golfers take advantage of practice facilities to adapt to Mediterranean conditions, where sea wind can significantly alter ball behavior. Local instructors are familiar with these climatic variables and offer invaluable advice.

## Frequently asked questions about golf in Pals

**Do you need to be a member to play at Golf de Pals?**
The course welcomes visitors year-round, subject to availability. In high season, tee times fill up fast: booking at least a week in advance is recommended.

**What is the average green fee at Pals?**
Green fees range from 60 to 120 euros depending on the season and tee time. Golf d'Empordà tends to be slightly cheaper, especially in low season.

**Can you rent equipment on site?**
Both courses have a pro shop with club, trolley and buggy hire. The rental equipment quality is fine for a discovery round.

**When is the best season for golf in the Empordà?**
Spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) offer the best conditions: pleasant temperatures, moderate wind and lower rates. Summer is still playable early in the morning.

**Is Golf de Pals suitable for beginners?**
The course is technical and can be challenging for beginners. Golf d'Empordà, with its two different layouts, offers more accessible options on the Forest course.

**How far are the courses from Aparthotel Arenal?**
Golf de Pals is less than a ten-minute drive. Golf d'Empordà is about fifteen minutes away. Both are reachable without tolls.
