Pals is best discovered on foot. The old village perched on its hillside overlooks the Baix Empordà plain, between the Montgrí massif and the Mediterranean Sea. You enter through the Torre de les Hores, a remnant of the 12th-century walls, and leave wanting to return.

## The medieval village

The historic center of Pals was painstakingly restored stone by stone beginning in the 1950s. The result is striking: narrow streets of dressed stone, Gothic arches, houses with sun-golden facades. The plaça Major hosts a market on Tuesday mornings from June to September.

The Josep Pla viewpoint, named after the Catalan writer native to the region, offers 360-degree views over the rice fields, the Illes Medes, and Montgrí. Sunset remains the best time to make the climb.

## The rice fields of Pals

The Empordà is one of Catalonia's few rice-growing areas. The rice fields of Pals stretch between the village and the sea, creating a unique landscape that changes color with the seasons. In spring, flooded fields reflect the sky. In summer, the intense green of the rice contrasts with the Mediterranean blue.

Pals rice has a local designation and appears on the menus of all restaurants in the region, particularly in arròs a la cassola, the emblematic dish of the Empordà.

## Beaches and nature

Platja de Pals extends more than 3 kilometers of fine sand, bordered by dunes and pine groves. Less crowded than the beaches of the southern coast, it retains a natural character that gives it charm. The dunes shelter protected flora and several hiking trails line the coastline.

Just a short drive away, the Sa Riera cove offers a more intimate alternative, with its rocks and crystalline waters.

## Gastronomy

The Empordà concentrates a remarkable density of quality restaurants. Pals boasts several recognized establishments, from gourmet restaurants to village bistros. Local products dominate the menus: Palamós shrimp, Pals rice, Escala anchovies, Empordà wines.

The Pals market and the larger Torroella de Montgrí market allow you to shop directly from local producers.

## From the Arenal

Aparthotel Arenal sits at the foot of the medieval village, a two-minute walk from the plaça Major. The beach is five minutes by car, Pals Golf course three minutes away. This central location makes it easy to explore all of the Empordà without relying solely on your car.
